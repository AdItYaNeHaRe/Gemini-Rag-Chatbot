Remote Work Policy - OS3 Infotech

Employees may work remotely up to 4 days per week, with Wednesday designated
as the mandatory in-office collaboration day for all engineering teams.

Expense Reimbursement

Employees working remotely are eligible for a one-time home office setup
reimbursement of up to INR 15,000, covering items such as a monitor, chair,
or desk. Claims must be submitted within 60 days of the purchase date along
with an itemized receipt.

Leave Policy

All full-time employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave and
12 days of sick leave per calendar year. Unused annual leave of up to 5 days
may be carried forward to the next calendar year; sick leave does not carry
forward.

Working Hours

Standard working hours are 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM IST, with a one-hour lunch
break. Flexible start times between 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM are permitted with
manager approval.
